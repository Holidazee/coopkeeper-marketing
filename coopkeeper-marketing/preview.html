How it works

Three steps. No onboarding wizard.

You can be logging your first egg in under a minute. Really.

  1. STEP 01

    Add your flock

    Start with a name or two. Breed, age, and notes are optional. No setup gauntlet — just tell us who's in the coop.

  2. STEP 02

    Log daily

    A tap for eggs in the morning. A quick entry after cleaning. Receipts go in when you feel like it. That's the whole routine.

  3. STEP 03

    See trends

    Weekly and monthly views show what's working — which hens are crushing it, when you're overspending, when the coop's overdue.

FAQ

Questions people actually ask.

Still have a question? We'd love to hear it — it's probably something other keepers are wondering too.

  • Is it free?

    Yep, fully free while we're in early access. If that ever changes, you'll know well in advance — and your data always stays yours to export.

  • Does it work on my phone?

    CoopKeeper is built mobile-first. Add it to your home screen and it behaves like an app on iOS and Android — no install required.

  • Can I track multiple coops?

    You can track as many birds as you want, grouped however you like — separate flocks, separate coops, no problem. Everything lives on one dashboard.

  • What about roosters or hens that aren't laying?

    They're part of the family too. Track them without any pressure to log eggs — they just live in your flock view and count toward your chicken total.

  • Do I need internet at the coop?

    You only need internet to sign in. Day-to-day logging is lightweight, so a spotty connection at the coop is fine. Offline mode is on the roadmap.

  • Do you sell my data?

    Never. Your flock is your business. We don't sell, share, or train models on your data — and you can export or delete everything, any time.
